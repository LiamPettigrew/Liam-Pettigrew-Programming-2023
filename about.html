Welcome to FreeTriviaSet.com

FreeTriviaSet.com is a website that anyone can access for free, with no signups, memberships, or advertisements. FreeTriviaSet.com has a range of ten different categories of trivia questions, all with multiple levels of difficulty. Every level has ten questions, all with four multiple-choice answers. The user also has the option to enable a ten-second timer for every question, and if the timer runs out the user cannot answer. The user’s selected answer may be correct or incorrect, and the button will light up with green or red after answering. The total results will be shown to the user once they’ve finished the quiz. The website is designed to be easy-to-use, accessible, and robust.

All answers to questions have been gathered from official sources. Please contact 19026@fraser.school.nz if you wish to request where any information was found.
